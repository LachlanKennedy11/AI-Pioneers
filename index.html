The Pioneers of Artificial Intelligence

What does it mean to be an AI pioneer? It means having the vision to imagine a future where machines can think and learn, and the courage to build the very first foundations of that future. These pioneers are the innovators, mathematicians, and engineers who, through their groundbreaking work, have created the tools, theories, and concepts that have shaped the field of artificial intelligence as we know it today.
